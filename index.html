Questions for Care (Q4C) is a healthcare demonstrator combining an industry standard (HL7 FHIR), a JavaScript framework (AngularJS), and Scalable Vector Graphic animation (SnapSVG). See the Rhizome repositiory.

Timelines for Care

Click "Result" or "Edit in jsFiddle" to view demonstrator.

Timelines for Care (T4C) is a healthcare demonstrator combining an industry standard (HL7 FHIR), a JavaScript timeline tool (TimelineJS), and some simple data adaptation. See the Rhizome repositiory.

My goal is to eventually combine these two demonstrations into a single application that will use Healthcare Questionnaires to determine outcomes, and then assign appropriate CarePlans based on these outcomes. For instance, if your responses place you in a high risk category, a CarePlan is activated which includes contacting a practitioner and following through with additional steps at home.
